ChargeWarner lets you minimize the risks for battery explosion and fire.

Are you worried your battery is going to explode, and your home catch fire? ChargeWarner will help you, by playing a warning tone at important occasions:

  • When the battery is fully charged, so you don't charge the battery longer than needed.
  • When the battery temperature gets high, as this could be a sign that the battery will explode.
  • If you have the charger plugged late at night, so you don't have the charger applied while you are asleep.
ChargeWarner is designed to use very little battery, and only performs checks when the charger is plugged. ChargeWarner is a free app, but will show some ads after it has been used for a while.
